Egg Roll Recipe

Description

Korean Egg Roll Kimbap is a popular Korean dish that combines the flavors of egg rolls and kimbap. It is a delicious and versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a snack, lunch, or picnic food. The dish features a seasoned egg omelet rolled with a variety of fillings, such as vegetables, pickled radish, and sometimes ham or sausage.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 4 sheets of roasted seaweed (gim/nori)
  • 1 cup cooked rice, seasoned with salt
  • 1/2 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup julienned cucumber
  • 1/2 cup julienned pickled radish (danmuji)
  • 4 slices of ham or cooked sausage (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with salt and black pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat and add the vegetable oil.
  3. Pour a quarter of the egg mixture into the pan, tilting it to spread the egg evenly.
  4. Cook the egg until it sets, about 2-3 minutes, then gently roll it up from one side of the pan to the other.
  5. Push the rolled egg to one side of the pan and repeat the process with the remaining egg mixture until you have four rolled omelets.
  6. Allow the rolled omelets to cool slightly, then slice them into thin strips.
  7. Place a sheet of roasted seaweed (gim/nori) on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean surface.
  8. Spread a quarter of the seasoned rice evenly over the seaweed, leaving a small border at the top.
  9. Arrange a quarter of the egg strips, julienned carrots, cucumber, pickled radish, and ham or sausage (if using) on top of the rice.
  10. Using the bamboo sushi mat or your hands, tightly roll the kimbap, applying gentle pressure to secure the filling.
  11. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients to make three more rolls.
  12. Once the kimbap rolls are tightly wrapped, slice them into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  13. Serve the Korean Egg Roll Kimbap as is or with a side of soy sauce or Korean dipping sauce.
